Our goal

According to the Allergy Asthma Center of Austin, the capital of Texas often finds itself within the top 5 worst places for allergies in the country. Knowing about your allergens and exactly when they are high is key to staying safe. Our goal is to make Austin a more allergy-aware community and allow Austinites to enjoy this beautiful city without the endless suffering that allergies bring.

All About: Mold




SOURCE: Mold is a fungus which produces spores that when inhaled, cause allergy symptoms.



TIMES OF THE YEAR: High after rain.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES: Avoid going outside or wear a mask during rainy days. Additionally, frequently check to ensure there is no standing water within your home.

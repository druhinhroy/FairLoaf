All About: Ash




SOURCE: Ash pollen comes from the flowers of the Ash Tree, and is spread by wind.



TIMES OF THE YEAR: Worst from February to early June.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES: Avoid the source of pollen entirely, keep windows closed, and wash your hair after spening a long time outside.

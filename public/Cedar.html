All About: Cedar




SOURCE: Cedar pollen is created by male juniper trees, which look like Christmas Trees.



TIMES OF THE YEAR: Winter months.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES: Cedar is difficult to avoid, so wear a mask when spending a long time outside. Additionally, start taking allergy medicine like Claritin around mid-November.

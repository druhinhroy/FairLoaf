All About: Pecan




SOURCE: Elm pollen is created by the flowers of the elm tree, which is then carried by the wind.



TIMES OF THE YEAR: Elm pollen is worst at the end of February and early March, during pollinating season for the trees.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES: Avoid the tree entirely. Elm trees are often planted in lawns, so make sure your house doesn't have them.

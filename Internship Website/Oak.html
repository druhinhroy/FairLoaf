All About: Oak




SOURCE: Oak pollen is spread when old Oak Tree leaves shed their catkins, which are dead flowers that then release their pollen.



TIMES OF THE YEAR: February to early June.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES: Avoid the oak pollen piles that form during Oak season. Make sure oak trees are not planted in your lawn.

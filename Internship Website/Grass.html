All About: Grass




SOURCE: Grass pollen comes from Bermuda Grass, which is often used in lawns, and Johnson Grass. The pollen comes from flowers and weeds which can grow on the grass.



TIMES OF THE YEAR: March to September.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES: Regularly cut your grass to prevent pollen producing-flowers and weeds from growing.

All About: Mulberry




SOURCE: Mulberry pollen is created by the flowers and fruits of the Mulberry tree, which is then carried by the wind.



TIMES OF THE YEAR: Mulberry pollen is worst in March and April.



PREVENTATIVE MEASURES: Mulberry trees are not generally planted much. Basic preventative measures like keeping windows closed and washing your hands and face after going outside should suffice to stay safe.
